About Me

I am a Ph.D. Student studying Computer Science at the University of South Florida. I work with Dr. John Licato in the Advancing Machine and Human Reasoning Lab, where my work has broadly focused on natural language inference and computational psycholinguistics.

I am also a member of Turkic Interlingua, where I work on developing lanuage models for low resource Turkic languages.
